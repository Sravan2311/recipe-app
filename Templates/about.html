About

Recipe Generation from Food Image

This project introduces an Inverse Cooking System that automatically generates a detailed recipe from a single food image. Using advanced deep learning models, the system first predicts ingredients as unordered sets, capturing their dependencies without enforcing a strict sequence. It then generates step-by-step cooking instructions by jointly attending to both the food image and the predicted ingredients.

Key Features
  • Predicts ingredients directly from a food image
  • Generates detailed cooking instructions
  • Uses a novel deep learning architecture for unordered ingredient prediction
  • Simultaneous attention to both image and ingredient data
Vision

Our vision is to bridge the gap between food photography and culinary knowledge, making it possible for anyone to recreate delicious dishes simply by capturing an image.
